About waterborne diseases

Waterborne illnesses spread via contaminated water. Quick facts:

  • Common causes: fecal contamination, poor sanitation, unsafe drinking sources.
  • Symptoms: diarrhea, vomiting, fever — monitor vulnerable groups (children, elderly).
  • Prevention: boiling, chlorination, safe storage, improved sanitation & hygiene.

This platform helps capture local test results and health reports so officials can act quickly.
